Chicken Sandwich

Description


Its the best chicken sandwich I've made .


Ingredients


  • 1 ½ tablespoons sweet pickle relish
  • 8 slices sourdough bread
  • 1 cup torn lettuce
  • 1 tablespoon canned chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, seeded and minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon white sugar
  • 4 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons chopped green onion

Steps

  1. Butter your bread and toast them until golden while you chop the onion and garlic.
    Take onion to a small bowl and squeeze up a bit of lemon juice.
    You can also add more veggies like chopped bell peppers, cucumbers & tomatoes.
  2. Add oil and saute garlic
  3. Add chicken and begin sautee
  4. Add salt and wait for sautee to complete
  5. Add Red chili powder and garam masala
  6. While the chicken cooks, add mayo, gound mustard and crushed pepper
  7. Add onions and parsley
  8. Mix everything and add some lemon juice
  9. Add the chicken
  10. mix the chicken
  11. Enjoy!
